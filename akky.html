The desire to be unhappy
From every corner of humanity, it seems that sadness is not just something to be overcome, but also something that is desired.
The extensive trope of misery in literature and art conditions us to believe that if we havent endured enough suffering, then we havent lived a life worthy of inspiration.
Almost every protagonist in every story seems to have a tragic past. It doesnt have to be so, but it is. And every one wants to be a protagonist.
